§1 Vision & Positioning
愿景与定位

Target Audience: Software architects, technical leads, and senior engineers who want a structured, visual knowledge system that connects classical engineering wisdom to AI-era practice. Not beginners — this series assumes 5+ years of industry experience.

目标读者：软件架构师、技术负责人和资深工程师，希望建立连接经典工程智慧与AI时代实践的结构化视觉知识体系。非入门级——假设5年以上行业经验。

Core Thesis: Classical software engineering books are not obsolete in the AI era — they are the foundation that makes AI augmentation effective. Every article in this series follows a dual axis: grounded in established book knowledge, extended through the lens of AI co-evolution. The architect who has read Martin, Fowler, Kleppmann, and Evans will outperform an AI-only practitioner because they bring judgment that no model can replicate.

核心论点：经典软件工程书籍在AI时代并未过时——它们是使AI增强有效的基础。每篇文章沿双轴展开：扎根于成熟的书本知识，通过AI协同进化的视角延伸。读过Martin、Fowler、Kleppmann和Evans的架构师将胜过纯AI实践者，因为他们带来模型无法复制的判断力。

Unique Value Proposition: Unlike generic "top 10 books" lists, this series synthesizes multiple books per topic, resolves contradictions between authors, and projects each topic forward into the 2026-2030 AI horizon. Unlike pure AI trend reports, every claim is anchored in battle-tested engineering principles. The output is not just "content" — it's a reusable, RAG-ready knowledge base for architectural decision-making.

独特价值主张：与泛泛的"十大书单"不同，本系列每个主题综合多本书，解决作者之间的矛盾，并将每个主题投射到2026-2030的AI地平线。与纯AI趋势报告不同，每个观点都锚定在经过验证的工程原则上。产出不仅是"内容"——而是可复用的、RAG就绪的架构决策知识库。

§2 Article Structure Template
文章结构模板 · 每篇文章的六段式骨架

Every article follows the same six-section skeleton. This consistency creates a predictable reading experience across the 10-month series — readers know where to find what they need.

01
Core Insight — The Thesis 核心洞察
One big idea in 60 seconds. The mental model shift. If a reader only reads this section, they still walk away with the key takeaway. Includes the Hero Diagram — the one picture that captures the entire article's concept.
02
Classical Foundation — Source Books 经典基础
3-4 books that ground the topic. Book cards with title, author, key insight, and which spectrum stage they cover. This section answers "why should I trust this framework?" before any AI claims appear.
03
AI Co-Evolution — Four Angles AI协同进化
4 concrete angles where AI reshapes the topic. Each follows: classical principle → AI-era extension → concrete example (code, diagram, or real-world case). This is the novel contribution of the series.
04
Decision Table 决策表
6-8 row table: Scenario | Classical Approach | AI-Augmented Approach. The "what do I do Monday morning?" deliverable. Designed to be screenshot-able and shareable with teams.
05
Practical Checklist 实践检查表
10-12 concrete action items tagged by maturity stage. The "take it back to your team" deliverable. Ordered by dependency — don't skip stages.
06
Architect's Takeaway 架构师要点
Close the loop back to the thesis. Reinforce "both/and" message. Tease the next article. 2-3 paragraphs max.

Bilingual Convention: English paragraphs carry the full argument. Chinese appears as italicized notes below each paragraph — compressed, essential, serving as comprehension anchor. Not duplication; distillation.

双语约定：英文段落承载完整论证。中文以斜体注释出现在每段下方——压缩的、精华的、作为理解锚点。不是复制而是蒸馏。

Phase Strategy 阶段策略

Phase 1 (M1-M2): Code Craft — start with the most concrete, individually actionable content. Low dependency on other articles. Establishes the visual style and bilingual format.

Phase 2 (M3-M4): Design Thinking — builds on L0 patterns, introduces DDD and API design. NRS-specific examples become prominent. Requires L0 concepts as foundation.

Phase 3 (M5-M7): Architecture — the densest phase (5 articles). Depends heavily on L0 (code quality) and L1 (patterns, DDD). This is the "main event" of the series.

Phase 4 (M7-M9): Infrastructure — cross-references L2 architecture decisions. The CI/CD article (3.1) is the bridge between architecture and infrastructure.

Phase 5 (M9-M10): Engineering Culture — the capstone. Synthesizes all prior layers into organizational and personal practice. Article 4.3 is the meta-conclusion.

§7 Output Formats & Distribution
输出格式与分发

Three Output Channels 三个输出渠道

Channel A — HTML Articles: The primary format. Interactive SVG diagrams, responsive layout, dark terminal aesthetic. Self-contained single-file HTML (no external dependencies beyond Google Fonts). Can be hosted as a static site or shared as files.

Channel B — PowerPoint Decks: One deck per article for team presentations and IT meetings. Uses established gen_slide3.js style: LAYOUT_WIDE, white bg (#F8FAFC), blue header (#1B6CA8), 4-column card layout, Calibri + Courier New, English-first with Chinese in parentheses.

Channel C — Markdown in Git: Each article as .md in a Git repository following the .ai/ directory structure. This format is RAG-ready — Claude or other AI agents can retrieve and reference articles during architectural decision-making.

Per-Article Deliverables 每篇交付物

Each article produces: (1) one HTML file (~15-20KB, self-contained), (2) one .pptx deck (8-12 slides), (3) one .md file (RAG-ready), (4) 3-5 SVG diagrams (extractable as standalone assets). Total series output: 20 HTML files + 20 decks + 20 markdown files + 80+ diagrams.
