# arch-spec

**Classical Foundations × AI Co-Evolution — An Illustrated Knowledge Base for Software Architects**

经典基础 × AI协同进化 — 面向软件架构师的图文知识库

---

## What is this?

A 20-article illustrated series that connects classical software engineering wisdom to AI-era practice. Each article follows a dual axis: grounded in established book knowledge, extended through the lens of AI co-evolution.

The core thesis: classical software engineering books are not obsolete — they are the **foundation** that makes AI augmentation effective.

## Structure

```
Layer 0  Code Craft        4 articles   The engineer who sees code as infrastructure
Layer 1  Design Thinking   4 articles   The engineer who designs for two audiences
Layer 2  Architecture      5 articles   The engineer who quantifies trade-offs
Layer 3  Infrastructure    4 articles   The engineer who builds the machine that watches the machine
Layer 4  Eng. Culture      3 articles   The engineer who leads the transition
```

## Article Format

Every article follows a six-section skeleton:

1. **Core Insight** — one big idea + hero diagram
2. **Classical Foundation** — 3-4 source books
3. **AI Co-Evolution** — 4 angles (classical principle → AI extension → example)
4. **Decision Table** — Classical vs. AI-Augmented approaches
5. **Practical Checklist** — concrete actions tagged by maturity stage
6. **Architect's Takeaway** — synthesis + next article teaser

Bilingual: English primary, Chinese supplementary (italic notes after each paragraph).

## Design

Dark terminal aesthetic with semantic color system. Self-contained HTML articles. See `templates/article-base.css` for the design system and `.ai/style-guide.md` for documentation.

## Source Books

30+ books across 6 core series: Uncle Bob Clean Series, Martin Fowler Lineage, Richards & Ford Architecture Trilogy, DDIA + System Design, DDD Arc, Google Engineering. See `plan/series-plan.html` for the full registry.
